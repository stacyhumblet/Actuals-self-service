Data Caveats and Nuances

  • Data is dummy and for testing purposes only.
  • Worklog hours may not reflect actual time spent due to rounding.
  • Some entries may have missing roadmap mappings (orphans).
  • Hierarchy levels are simulated and may not match real organizational structure.
  • Issue keys are randomly generated and may not correspond to real tickets.
  • Account keys follow a specific format but are fictional.
  • Data covers 2024-2026 with weighted distribution towards recent periods.
  • Filtering is case-sensitive for text fields.
  • Downloads are limited to filtered results only.
